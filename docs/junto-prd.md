## What Junto is

Junto helps student builders find the people they need to move their ideas forward.

## The product

A feed of actionable things on your campus.

It looks like a normal vertical feed, and that's fine. What makes it work isn't the layout, it's that **everything in the feed is something you can act on, and every tap leads to a real connection.** No memes, no clout, no generic posts.

## Card types

The feed is made of four cards:

- **Ask** — someone posts a need ("need a dev for a new feature"). You offer to help / reach out.
- **Opportunity** — an event, program, or thing to apply for, usually from an org (e.g. Open Pitch Night). You RSVP / show up / apply.
- **Match** — system-generated. Junto pairs your skills to someone's Ask. You connect.
- **Update** — a progress post. You react.

There is **no standalone Offer card.** "What I can help with" lives on your profile. The system turns that into **Matches** when it fits someone's Ask, so offers show up already targeted instead of as noise.

## How a card behaves

- The list is a clean scannable surface. **No buttons in the row.**
- Tap a card → detail view → that's where you act (offer to help, reach out, RSVP, react).
- **Asks show recency** ("2h"). **Opportunities show the real date/time.** Don't force "open/closed" status labels.

## Creating

- Floating **+** → create a post (Ask or Update). Prompt: *"What do you need right now?"*
- Center nav icon = **Ask Junto** — the AI assistant. Ask it anything.

## Navigation (5 tabs)

1. **Home** — the feed
2. **Search / Discover** — people, projects, events, resources
3. **Ask Junto** (center) — AI assistant
4. **Messages** — intros + conversations
5. **Activity** — matches, responses, reactions

Profile exists but is not a tab. It supports trust and holds "what I can help with."

## The loop

1. Student posts an Ask (or the system surfaces a Match).
2. The right person sees it in their feed.
3. Someone responds / connects.
4. The student moves forward.
5. UNL sees what students need and where momentum is happening.

## Dashboard (UNL)

The university sees ecosystem intelligence, not social analytics. v1 is two views:

- **Pulse** — active asks, responses/intros, resolved needs, top needs this week.
- **Asks** — the queue: open / matched / resolved, by category, with suggested helpers.

People, Projects, Insights, and Reports come later.

## MVP lock

The smallest version that proves the loop:

> A student asks for help → the right person sees it → someone responds → the need moves toward resolution → UNL can see the ecosystem working.

**Student app v1:** Home · Discover · Ask Junto · Messages · Activity
**Dashboard v1:** Pulse · Asks
**Cards v1:** Ask · Opportunity · Match · Update
